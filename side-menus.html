This is an example of a side menu in the campaign builder flow. The header is an h2 because it is secondary to the title of the builder step.

Each list item is separated with a white divider (consistent with the rules for dividers) and is large enough for touch. The selected item is highlighted in white to differentiate it from the unselected items. The active item also has a drop shadow and z-index of 1 to help increase contrast for accessibility purposes.

Unselected items use the standard link color for text and have hover states to indicate that they are actionable.

For a clean and streamlined UI, audience icons are aligned in a column. Numbers are right aligned, consistent with web standards.
